About Me

Hello! I'm Aitor Díez Mateo, a passionate Computer Science and Data Science student from Spain with a deep fascination for turning complex data into actionable insights. I believe in the power of technology to solve real-world problems and create meaningful impact.

Background

Ever since I was a kid, I’ve been fascinated by how the world works. I remember spending hours exploring ideas, asking questions, and enjoying the thrill of solving problems, whether it was a tricky math question or figuring out how things fit together. That constant curiosity has always been my driving force. I love learning, discovering new perspectives, and finding beauty in the process of understanding.

In school, my love for math naturally grew into a passion for bigger challenges, where patience and creativity go hand in hand. At the same time, technology started to catch my interest, not just as a tool, but as something that constantly reshapes how we live, connect, and understand the world. All of this naturally led me to the world of data. Analyzing information, understanding problems, and coming up with solutions that genuinely help people is something that really motivates me.

Beyond the analytical side, I’ve always loved connecting with people. I’m very social and really enjoy spending time with friends and family, making plans, and creating memories together. Working in a team is something I’m passionate about. I believe the best solutions come from combining different ideas and perspectives to reach a common goal.

And I can’t forget sports, which play a big part in my life. They help me stay active, release energy, and learn the importance of discipline and consistency, values I try to bring into everything I do.

All in all, I’d describe myself as curious, passionate, and social, always looking to learn, grow, and enjoy the journey, both personally and professionally.
